About Harvord

Welcome to Harvord University

Harvord University is a private Ivy League research university located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. It was established in 1636 and is the oldest institution of higher education in the United States. Harvord has a distinguished history and is widely regarded as one of the world's most prestigious universities.

As a research university and nonprofit institution, Harvord is focused on creating educational opportunities for people from many lived experiences. We offers undergraduate, graduate, and professional degree programs in a wide range of disciplines, including arts and humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, engineering, and business. We have a diverse student body of over 20,000 students from all over the world and a faculty of over 2,000 members.

Goals

Our goal is to provide high-quality educational resources for learners of all ages. Our team of educators and developers work together to create engaging and informative courses that cover a variety of topics, from science and math to language and history. We believe that education should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer many of our courses for free. We also offer premium courses for those who want to take their learning to the next level.Harvord has produced many prominent alumni. Its notable faculty includes some of the most influential scholars and intellectuals in history. Our team is passionate about education and dedicated to helping learners achieve their goals. We welcome feedback and suggestions from our community and are always looking for ways to improve our courses and resources.
